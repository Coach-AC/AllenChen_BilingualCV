About Me

I'm a Beijing-based international school educator with a decade-plus career spanning MYP Physical & Health Education, K–12 Athletic Administration, and large-scale Student Activities leadership. My work sits at the intersection of sport, education, and community — and I'm passionate about building environments where young athletes thrive.

From founding vertically aligned basketball programs at Keystone Academy to directing K–12 athletics and ASA systems at Haidian Kaiwen, I've consistently taken on roles that require both strategic vision and hands-on execution. I'm equally comfortable and capable of coaching a varsity team program, negotiating vendor contracts, or designing an inquiry-based PHE unit.

Outside of school, I lead Panthers Basketball Academy — one of Beijing's most active youth basketball communities — managing leagues, camps, invitationals, and school partnerships year-round. I'm also a father of two boys, which keeps me grounded in what really matters in youth development.
